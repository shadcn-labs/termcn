## 26 May 2026 - OpenTUI Support

We added OpenTUI support across the registry and documentation, making it possible to build and preview terminal UIs with OpenTUI alongside Ink.

## 16 April 2026 - More Themes

We expanded the terminal theme catalog to 30 themes, including [Amoled](/themes/amoled), [Aura](/themes/aura), [Ayu](/themes/ayu), and many more.

## 5 April 2026 - Initial Release

We shipped the initial release of termcn focused on Ink, with 113 components and templates in total and a separate theme system.

Follow [termcn on GitHub](https://github.com/termcn/termcn) for source history and future tagged releases.
